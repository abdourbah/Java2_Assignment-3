Method Name: countScoresAbove70()

Explanation:

This method counts how many scores that are above 70 was scored by an individual student. It loops through the scores and increases the counter each time a scored above 70 is met and then returns the total count.

Reason for Adding:

I simply added this method to check consistencies  or level of performance of each student in different subjects.
It will help know if a student has understand many of his or her courses or subjects by scoring above 70.
